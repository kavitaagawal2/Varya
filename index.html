The Kachi Ghani Story

The Ghani Hasn't Changed in 100 Years. Neither Has the Purity.

Kachi Ghani is the traditional way of extracting mustard oil — seeds crushed using a wooden or stone ghani, the way it's been done for generations, long before steam refining and chemical solvents took over the industry.

No chemicals. No shortcuts. Just seed, patience, and an age-old process.
